PodLP is the first and most popular podcast app for feature phones, available on [KaiOS](https://www.kaiostech.com/store/apps/?bundle_id=com.podlp.podlp), the JioPhone, and Cloud Phone. With **10+ million installs** across more than **175 countries**, PodLP brings the podcast listening experience to the next billion listeners on affordable devices.

{{< br >}}
PodLP was a [2020 KaiOS Staff Pick](https://www.kaiostech.com/discover-podlp-the-leading-podcast-app-for-kaios-smart-feature-phones/), featured on [Podnews](https://podnews.net/update/kaios-podcast-app), [WBUR](https://www.wbur.org/citrus/2020/12/15/public-radio-future-digital-divide), and [Sounds Profitable](https://soundsprofitable.com/the-download/improving-accessibility/). You can also listen to podcasts discussing PodLP including [Buzzcast](https://www.buzzsprout.com/231452/episodes/4419371-india-s-newest-podcast-app-magic-mastering-update), [Svelte Society](https://sveltesociety.dev), and [Podcasting Tech](https://www.podcastingtech.com/tom-barrasso-reveals-how-podlp-unlocks-3b-listeners-beyond-smartphones/).

{{< br >}}
## Labs & Libraries

{{< br >}}
PodLP has branched out into experiments like [PodCal](https://podcal.app/), a tool to subscribe to podcasts in your calendar app, an NPM package to parse RSS feeds called [`podparse`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/podparse), and feature suggestions for the [Podcast Index](https://github.com/Podcastindex-org/podcast-namespace/issues/174) XML namespace.
